About Me

I graduated magna cum laude from Columbia University in 2016 with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Philosophy and Music. Having completed my liberal education (to my current satisfaction, at any rate), I am now pursuing my technical education.

My present ambition is to learn to code in three months ... with no prior experience whatsoever. Though it may be a long shot, my goal is to land an IT job by this summer.

My current coding-related interests skew toward AI programming and video game development. But all of that could quite easily change as I quickly learn more concepts in UC Berkeley Extension's full-time, full-stack, full-night's-sleep-depriving web development boot camp.
